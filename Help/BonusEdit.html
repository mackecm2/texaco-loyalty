Bonus Edit

  • Product Value is for promotions for spend on one product*.
  • Product Quantity is for per litre or per mars bar*.
  • Department is not currently implemented.
  • Period Spend For personal campaign bonuses envolving a minimum spend in a period.
  • Total Spend is for bonuses that use the total value of the transaction.
  • Visit is for bonuses not related to spend value in any way.
    * These two form one area.
    All bonuses are applied in order with any bonus marked as excluded having the relevant spend excluded from other calculations.
    This means that if you have a double bonus not marked as excluded then the double will be applied and then the standard giving a triple bonus.
