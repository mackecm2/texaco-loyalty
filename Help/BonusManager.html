Bonus Management

Several areas interact here to bring you a flexiable and powerful promotion managment wizard the downside being that if you get this wrong it can cause people to get bonuses they didn't expect or people to not get bonuses they did.
On the manage screen you will see a sumary of the details of all the bonuses active in the system. Different bonuses apply to different areas that and you can have one bonus Hit in each area for a single transaction. The areas are:-.
  • Product Value is for promotions for spend on one product*.
  • Product Quantity is for per litre or per mars bar promotions*.
  • Department is not currently implemented.
  • Total Spend is for bonuses that use the total value of the transaction.
  • Visit is for bonuses not related to spend value in any way.
    * These two form one area.

    Priority Management

    The first bonus to be applicable in an area is applied so the order of testing is important.
    For this reason you can use the up and down arrows to alter the priority.
