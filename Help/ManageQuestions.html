Editing a question

Manage Question

  • To change the priority of a question use the up/down arrows.
  • A maximum of 6 of the highest priority questions that haven't been answered, or whose answers have expired, will be presented to the operator to ask the user.
  • To de-activate a question, uncheck the Active box. This does not delete any data.
  • To Edit a question, click on the list entry.

  • After completing all the changes to the list press the button.
  • To create a new question, press the button.
